Board of Advisors

Meet the experts guiding us toward innovation and excellence.

Venkat Panjani

Venkat Panjani is an accomplished technology expert specializing in cloud computing, disaster recovery, and cyber recovery solutions. He has extensive experience in Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Application Delivery Controller (ADC) technologies, leading initiatives in system verification, design validation, and quality assurance. Venkat's expertise in storage replication technologies such as EMC SRDF and Hitachi TC/UR, as well as native database replication for Oracle and MSSQL, has made him a leader in his field. As the Associate Director of Solutions Design Architecture, he is passionate about leveraging cloud solutions to enhance business continuity, ensuring ThinkOMega stays at the forefront of technology.

Venu Chitvel

Venu Chitvel is an experienced database architect with a diverse career spanning EMI, Ask.com, the Chicago Tribune, 1-800-Flowers, and BPS. He has made significant contributions to federal projects, as well as with Match Group and Experian Health. With an MS in IT and Cybersecurity from the University of Dallas, Venu is dedicated to designing secure, scalable database solutions for high-demand environments. His expertise is instrumental in ensuring the integrity and scalability of ThinkOMega's projects.
